操作界面示例截图（按步骤依次操作）

### 安全级别
&emsp;![1](../assets/img/dataSecurity/operationDetails/securitylevel/1.png)
&emsp;![2](../assets/img/dataSecurity/operationDetails/securitylevel/2.png)
&emsp;![3](../assets/img/dataSecurity/operationDetails/securitylevel/3.png)
&emsp;![4](../assets/img/dataSecurity/operationDetails/securitylevel/4.png)
&emsp;![5](../assets/img/dataSecurity/operationDetails/securitylevel/5.png)

&emsp;1. 进入数据安全-安全级别页面\
&emsp;2. 点击+，新建行业，输入行业名称和行业描述后，点击确定\
&emsp;3. 选中新建的行业，点击新建按钮\
&emsp;4. 填写完成的数据后点击确定按钮，成功新建安全级别\
&emsp;5. 已有的安全级别可编辑和删除
